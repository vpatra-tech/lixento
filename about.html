About ChromaShield Coatings

25 Years of Manufacturing Excellence in Industrial Coatings

Our Story

Built on Chemistry. Driven by Quality.

Founded in 1999, ChromaShield Coatings began as a small solvent-based paint producer in Mumbai's industrial belt. Over two and a half decades, we've grown into one of India's most trusted B2B paint and coatings manufacturers, serving clients across construction, infrastructure, oil & gas, marine, automotive, and consumer segments.

We have consistently invested in R&D, modern manufacturing infrastructure, and quality systems to stay ahead of industry demands — offering our clients not just products, but complete coating solutions.

Vision & Mission

Innovation & Quality

Research, Development & Quality Control

Our in-house R&D centre employs over 20 qualified chemists and material scientists who work continuously on formulation improvement, new product development, and customer-specific solutions.

  • Physical & chemical property testing per IS/ISO standards
  • Salt spray (ASTM B117), UV weathering & accelerated aging
  • Adhesion, flexibility, impact resistance testing
  • VOC analysis and environmental compliance checks
  • Batch-by-batch QC before dispatch
