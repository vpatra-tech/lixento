Building Industrial Coatings Excellence Since 1975

INDUSTRIA combines deep materials science expertise with large-scale manufacturing to deliver coatings that extend asset life, reduce maintenance cost and support circular, low-carbon operations.

Key Product Categories

Decorative Coatings

High-opacity, fade-resistant paints for residential and commercial interiors and exteriors with low-VOC formulations.

Industrial Coatings

Solvent-borne enamel and epoxy systems formulated for plant equipment, machinery and heavy industrial environments.

Automotive Coatings

Advanced finishes and primers meeting OEM specifications for appearance, corrosion and chemical resistance.

Protective Coatings

Long-life corrosion and abrasion resistant systems for infrastructure, marine and oil & gas applications.
