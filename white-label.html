What We Offer

White Label Services Included

Custom Formulation

Proprietary formulations matched to your specification — sheen level, viscosity, drying time, VOC levels, and performance benchmarks.

Colour Matching

Spectrophotometric colour matching for any RAL, NCS, or custom shade. Shade cards and swatch books available.

Packaging Solutions

1L, 4L, 10L, 20L cans; 200L drums; IBC totes. Label printing and shrink-sleeve application included in service.

Documentation

SDS, TDS, CoA, and test reports provided with every batch under your brand name for regulatory compliance.

NDA Protected

All formulations and business information protected under a comprehensive Non-Disclosure Agreement. Exclusivity options available.

Export Ready

GS1 barcoding, REACH-compliant SDS in multiple languages, and export documentation for EU, Middle East, and Southeast Asia.
